ACADEMICS

Session 

Academic Session Begins every year 
in the month of April i.e first week of Baisakh


Exams

Each Session is divided into 3 terms
with a terminal exams. First Terminal Exam happens in month of Asad-Shrawan, Second in Kartik-Mangsir and Final in month of Chaitra

Teaching & Non-Teaching Staff

We have 30 teaching staffs and 5 non-teaching staffs. All the teaching and non teaching staffs are very supportive towards students and help them grow and learn things. We also believe in having mutual respect between them.

Classes

We have classes from Nursery to Grade 10.
Each Class has a single section and number of student is no more than 50 as we believe in providing quality education.

school

Facilities

We have a well equipped computer lab and all other learning facilities that is required by secondary school children. 

Scholarship

Scholarship is provided to talented students as well as needy students. Scholarship is also provided on the basis of academic performances.
