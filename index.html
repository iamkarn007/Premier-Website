Premier Sec. School was established in the year 1998 A.D 2055 B.S. We have come a long way since then. We believe in providing quality education & it has been our top priority since the beginning. We provide an environment where one can learn a lot of basic skills. There is proverb “God helps those who help themselves”. That is what we believe in. Rather than spoon feeding the children we believe in teaching them real world things and helping them develop self-confidence that helps in improving personality and also bring satisfaction to the mind.

The world has an abundance of knowledge and information. In school, we study different subjects that cover history, biology, arts, literature, math, physics, physical education, geography, and many more. This should help us become aware of the hard work and efforts of our ancestors, the struggles of people from centuries ago, all the wars and battles that shaped our country, the beauty and intricacies of science and technology, and so on.
